How to create a TTreeView without scrollbars

If got here by following a link such as http://delphidabbler.com/tips/999 (where 999 is a number) then you were re-directed because the tip is not yet available on the new delphidabbler.com website. Go back to the delphidabbler.com tips page

Question
I want to use a TreeView (with more visible items than its window can show), but I don't need its scrollbars. How to do that?

Derive a new control from TTreeView and override its CreateParams method like this: 

uses
  CommCtrl;

procedure TNoScrollbarsTreeview.CreateParams(var Params: TCreateParams);
begin
  inherited;
  Params.style := Params.style or TVS_NOSCROLL;
end;
